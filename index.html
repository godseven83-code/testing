Frequently Asked Questions

What is the ESP8266 used for?

The ESP8266 is a powerful Wi-Fi microcontroller used for IoT projects, penetration testing, and educational purposes. It can be programmed for keystroke injection, payload delivery, and security testing scenarios.

Is the ESP8266 legal to use?

Yes, the ESP8266 is completely legal. However, it should only be used for authorized security testing, educational purposes, or on systems you own. Unauthorized use may violate local laws.
